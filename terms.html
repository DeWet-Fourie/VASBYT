1. Product information

Product images, prices, availability and specifications are provided for catalogue and enquiry purposes. Final stock, fitment, courier costs and payment details are confirmed before an order is completed.

2. Orders and payment

Orders are completed through WhatsApp, email, EFT or a secure payment link after availability has been confirmed. A product added to a quote is not reserved until the store confirms the order.

3. Returns and exchanges

Returns are subject to the store’s approval, product condition and supplier terms. Customers should contact the store within the stated return period with proof of purchase and photographs where relevant.

4. Fitment and compatibility

Vehicle-related products may require fitment confirmation. Customers are responsible for supplying accurate vehicle details before purchasing fitment-sensitive products.

5. Delivery

Nationwide courier may be available. Delivery times and costs depend on product size, destination, stock location and courier availability.

6. Privacy policy

Customer details submitted through WhatsApp, email or forms are used for enquiry response, order handling and support. These details should not be sold or shared unnecessarily.

7. Warranty

Warranty terms vary by brand or supplier. The store will assist with warranty enquiries where applicable, subject to supplier approval.
